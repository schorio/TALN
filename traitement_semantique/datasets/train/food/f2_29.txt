These are arguably the most versatile and nutritious of 
all types of vegetables. They are grown as part of other 
vegetables, such as beets and pumpkins, and as the leaves 
themselves. They contain dietary fibre, lots of vitamins and 
minerals, are low in calories, and have been associated 
with various health benefits36. Leafy greens are typically 
fast-growing and, eaten cooked or raw, are part of a wide 
variety of dishes all over the world.
